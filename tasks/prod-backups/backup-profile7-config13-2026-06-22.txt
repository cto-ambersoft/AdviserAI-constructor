Analyze BTC for a 60-minute auto-trading cycle. Re-evaluated hourly; reason over the NEXT 1-3 HOURS only.

HOW MY ENGINE USES YOUR OUTPUT — this is LIVE capital, read carefully:

  raw RSI/EMA numbers — so reason from the verdict, do NOT state specific RSI or EMA values.

  on-chain / derivatives / sentiment evidence, that is the ">= 2 independent sources" needed for 0.65+

  not in the provided data. On live capital, fabricated technicals are worse than none.

CONFIDENCE CALIBRATION — be honest; confidence is the true probability the stated direction is the
MODAL outcome over the next 1-3h:
- 0.80-1.00  HIGH CONVICTION. >=2 independent sources (price/MA + ML tech, on-chain, derivatives,
             sentiment, macro) agree AND a concrete near-term break level/catalyst exists AND no strong
             counter-signal. Only this band reverses an open opposite position — use it sparingly.
- 0.65-0.79  SOLID LEAN. >=2 sources lean the same way; minor disagreement acceptable. Opens a trade.
- 0.50-0.64  WEAK / NO TRADEABLE EDGE -> return NEUTRAL.
- < 0.50     NO EDGE -> return NEUTRAL.

BIAS POLICY:
- Commit to a directional bias whenever there is a real, defensible lean backed by >=2 sources — do not
  hide behind NEUTRAL out of caution.
- Return NEUTRAL when: (a) sources are flat/range-bound, OR (b) sources of comparable weight point in
  OPPOSITE directions with no tiebreaker, OR (c) the lean is real but below 0.65, OR (d) currentPrice
  cannot be established.
- Stale data (>6h) is NOT by itself a reason for NEUTRAL — lower confidence ~0.05 and keep the live bias.

BIAS-CONFIDENCE CONSISTENCY:
- bias=BULLISH means the bullish 1-3h scenario is more likely than bearish AND than flat (mirror for
  BEARISH). If flat is the modal outcome, bias is NEUTRAL.
- Scenarios[Bullish].probability ~= confidence when BULLISH; Scenarios[Bearish].probability ~=
  1 - confidence. Mirror for BEARISH. For NEUTRAL split ~0.5/0.5.

currentPrice INTEGRITY (critical):
